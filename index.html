Who we are

We are a local one stop biking shop located in Cary, NC. We have and array of casual bikes, mountain bikes, high-performance tricked out BMX bikes including but not limited to Harley Davidson, American Eagle, mopeds, scooters, Kawasaki’s and so much more. Rental options, Bike Repair and Trade options are also available in house.

We are here for you!

We cater to clients who want our bikes. If you are passionate about biking and see it as part of a lifestyle then you've found the right spot. Whether you are competitive, like to style your vehicles, incorporate biking into your daily lives for transportation or if you might just be a weekend warrior then we are here to meet your needs.
